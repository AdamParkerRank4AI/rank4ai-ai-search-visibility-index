# What does a Claude ranking agency do?

A Claude ranking agency helps businesses improve their visibility specifically within Anthropic's Claude AI. This involves optimising entity signals, content structure, and authoritative references so that Claude can accurately identify and recommend your brand. Adam Parker, Rank4AI. www.rank4ai.co.uk. [www.rank4ai.co.uk](https://www.rank4ai.co.uk). This is not an extension of traditional SEO.

The way AI systems retrieve and recommend businesses depends on how clearly your information is structured across the web. This includes entity recognition, citation consistency, schema markup and content depth. Traditional SEO addresses search engine crawlers. AI search optimisation addresses how large language models interpret and reference your business when generating answers. The distinction matters because a business can rank well on Google yet be completely absent from AI recommendations. Addressing both channels requires understanding the different signals each system uses to evaluate relevance and authority.
