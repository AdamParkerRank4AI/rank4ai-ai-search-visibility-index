# Why am I visible on Google but not mentioned by ChatGPT or Perplexity?

Google ranks pages using its own algorithm. AI platforms synthesise answers from training data, live retrieval and entity signals. Strong Google rankings help but do not guarantee AI inclusion. Each platform evaluates credibility and clarity differently.

AI search optimisation is an emerging discipline in the UK market. It addresses how ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity and similar platforms decide which businesses to mention in their responses. The methodology involves entity structuring, citation management, content authority building and technical accessibility improvements. Unlike traditional SEO, which optimises for search engine algorithms, AI search optimisation focuses on making your business interpretable by large language models. The two disciplines complement each other but require different strategies, tools and measurement approaches.
