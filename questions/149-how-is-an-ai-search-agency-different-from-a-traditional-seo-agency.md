# How is an AI search agency different from a traditional SEO agency?

A traditional SEO agency focuses on improving rankings in search engine results pages. An AI search agency focuses on how language models understand, describe, and recommend your business in conversational AI answers, which requires a different set of strategies and expertise.

Traditional SEO and AI search optimisation serve different purposes. SEO gets your website ranked in search engine results. AI search optimisation gets your business cited in AI-generated answers. Both are valuable, but the signals that drive each are different. Businesses that address both channels will have the broadest digital visibility. A comprehensive strategy treats traditional search and AI search as complementary channels. Investing in one does not replace the need for the other, and the businesses that perform best are those that maintain strong signals across both.
