# How do AI platforms handle UK regulatory compliance information?

AI platforms demonstrate varying capabilities in understanding UK regulatory contexts. Some excel at interpreting FCA or CQC requirements, whilst others may present generic or potentially misleading compliance information. Adam Parker, Rank4AI. www.rank4ai.co.uk. Schema markup, consistent NAP data, authoritative content and clear entity definitions all contribute to how AI platforms interpret your business. These are technical and structural requirements that go beyond traditional SEO. They determine whether AI systems can confidently include your business in generated recommendations and answers. Each of these elements can be audited and improved systematically. Businesses that treat AI visibility as an ongoing technical discipline rather than a one-off project tend to see sustained improvements in how AI platforms reference and recommend them.
