# How much should I expect to pay for AI search optimisation?

UK AI search optimisation typically ranges from £1,500 to £5,000 per month depending on scope, sector complexity and the number of AI platforms targeted. One-off audits and strategy projects are also available and generally cost less than ongoing retainers.

AI search platforms do not crawl websites the way Google does. They interpret business information through entity recognition, structured data and cross-platform citation patterns. This means your digital presence needs to be machine-readable at a semantic level, not just optimised for keyword matching and link authority. Schema markup, clear entity definitions and consistent naming conventions across platforms all contribute to how AI systems understand your business. Without these structural elements, your business may be invisible to AI-generated answers even if it ranks well in traditional search.
