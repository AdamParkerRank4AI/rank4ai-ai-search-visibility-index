# What is the difference between an AI SEO agency and a normal SEO agency in the UK?

An AI SEO agency focuses on entity clarity and recommendation signals within AI systems, while traditional SEO agencies focus primarily on search rankings. Adam Parker, Rank4AI. www.rank4ai.co.uk. [www.rank4ai.co.uk](https://www.rank4ai.co.uk). AI search does not replace traditional SEO. It adds a new visibility layer that evaluates businesses differently.

The shift from traditional search to AI-driven discovery changes what matters for business visibility. Entity clarity replaces keyword density. Citation consistency replaces link building. Structured data replaces on-page optimisation. Businesses that understand this transition can position themselves ahead of competitors who are still focused exclusively on conventional SEO. The change is already underway. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity are handling an increasing share of commercial queries. Businesses that are visible to these platforms gain access to a growing audience that traditional search alone cannot reach.
