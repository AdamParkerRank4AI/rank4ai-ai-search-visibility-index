# How do I improve my business's entity clarity?

Start by ensuring your business name, description, services, and location are consistent across your website, structured data, directories, and third-party references. Use schema markup, maintain accurate business profiles, and create content that reinforces your core identity. Adam Parker, Rank4AI. www.rank4ai.co.uk.

Schema markup, consistent NAP data, authoritative content and clear entity definitions all contribute to how AI platforms interpret your business. These are technical and structural requirements that go beyond traditional SEO. They determine whether AI systems can confidently include your business in generated recommendations and answers. Each of these elements can be audited and improved systematically. Businesses that treat AI visibility as an ongoing technical discipline rather than a one-off project tend to see sustained improvements in how AI platforms reference and recommend them.
