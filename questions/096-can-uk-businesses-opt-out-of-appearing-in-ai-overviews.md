# Can UK businesses opt out of appearing in AI Overviews?

There's no direct opt-out mechanism for AI Overviews specifically. However, businesses can use robots.txt or meta tags to prevent content indexing entirely, though this would affect all search visibility, not just AI Overviews. AI search platforms do not crawl websites the way Google does. They interpret business information through entity recognition, structured data and cross-platform citation patterns. This means your digital presence needs to be machine-readable at a semantic level, not just optimised for keyword matching and link authority. Schema markup, clear entity definitions and consistent naming conventions across platforms all contribute to how AI systems understand your business. Without these structural elements, your business may be invisible to AI-generated answers even if it ranks well in traditional search.
